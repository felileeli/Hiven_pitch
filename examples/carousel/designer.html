Amrita

Pratt Alumni

Amrita graduated from Pratt Instuitute and also completed the prestigious two-year executive training program at Bergdorf Goodman in New York City. She has worked with world-renowned designers such as Oscar de la Renta and Christian Lacroix. Recognized by her fellow designers, she was nominated by FGI for the Rising Star Award in the Fine Jewelry Category in 2006.

Viktoria

Pratt MArch Year One

An avid graphic designer turned architect, Viktoria was trained in the traditional Bauhaus with a clarity on typography and graphic design. Born and raised in Germany, her design is heavily influenced by the Bauhaus and as well as the Japanese minimalist design. While pursueing her degree, she enjoys her freetime working on illustration and graphic design.

Fai

MIT Alumni

A Hong Kong native designer and later educated in MIT, Fai's design emphasizes on the balance between science and beauty. While working in the city as an architectural designer, Fai likes spending his time researching on new products in 3d printing and has sources to 3d printing supplies and can deliver products with file support in a short turnover time.
